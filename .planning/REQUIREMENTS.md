# Requirements: Findo Sales Website

**Defined:** 2026-01-31
**Conversion Target:** 50% (from qualified referral traffic)
**Core Promise:** Zero risk, 2-minute setup, autonomous operation

---

## Conversion Science Framework

These requirements are organized around the **visitor's mental journey**, not pages or features.
Each requirement serves a specific psychological purpose in the conversion path.

**Traffic Context:** Pre-qualified referrals from partners, consultants, and satisfied customers.
Visitors arrive with partial trust and awareness — the website must CONFIRM and ACTIVATE.

---

## PHASE A: The 5-Second Victory (First Impression)

> "You have 5 seconds to prove you're what they came for."

The visitor arrives with a referral's recommendation in mind. In 5 seconds, they must:
1. Confirm "yes, this is what I was told about"
2. Understand what Findo does
3. See the path to action

### Requirements

- [ ] **5SEC-01**: Headline in Hebrew, under 8 words, states the core outcome
  - NOT what Findo is, but what the business owner GETS
  - Example: "לעולם לא תפספס עוד לקוח" (Never miss another customer)
  - Must pass 5-second test with 100% accuracy (3/3 testers)

- [ ] **5SEC-02**: Subheadline in 1 sentence explains the mechanism
  - HOW the outcome is delivered
  - Example: "Findo עונה לשיחות שפספסת, משיג ביקורות אוטומטית, ומנהל את הנוכחות שלך בגוגל - בלי שתעשה כלום"

- [ ] **5SEC-03**: Hero visual shows the product in action
  - NOT abstract graphics or stock photos
  - Shows WhatsApp conversation capturing a lead OR review being auto-replied
  - Animated or video, not static

- [ ] **5SEC-04**: Primary CTA visible without scrolling
  - Text: "התחל ניסיון חינם" or similar value-focused Hebrew
  - Color: Maximum contrast with background
  - Size: Minimum 48px height on mobile, 56px on desktop

- [ ] **5SEC-05**: Trust signal visible above fold
  - Either: Customer count ("500+ עסקים כבר משתמשים")
  - Or: Key metric ("הצלנו 12,000+ לידים החודש")
  - Or: Recognizable logo row

- [ ] **5SEC-06**: Zero visual noise competing for attention
  - One focal point (headline)
  - One action (CTA)
  - Supporting elements recede visually

- [ ] **5SEC-07**: Hebrew RTL layout feels native, not adapted
  - Visual weight on right side
  - Reading flow right-to-left
  - Icons/arrows direction-aware

**Certification Gate:** 5-second test with 3 real users. All 3 must correctly identify:
- What this is
- What they can do here
- Who it's for

## PHASE B: The Proof Cascade (Trust Building)

> "A claim without proof is just noise. Stack proof until doubt is impossible."

Referral provides initial trust. Website must AMPLIFY with overwhelming evidence.

### Requirements

- [ ] **PROOF-01**: Testimonial #1 - Full attribution
  - Real name, real photo, real business name
  - Specific metric: "הגדלנו את כמות הלידים ב-40%"
  - What nearly stopped them: addresses common objection
  - Hebrew, conversational tone

- [ ] **PROOF-02**: Testimonial #2 - Different business type
  - Different industry than #1
  - Different benefit emphasized
  - Shows breadth of applicability

- [ ] **PROOF-03**: Testimonial #3 - Emotional outcome
  - Focuses on feeling, not just metrics
  - "סוף סוף יש לי שקט נפשי" (Finally I have peace of mind)
  - Addresses the REAL reason they want this

- [ ] **PROOF-04**: Video testimonial
  - 30-60 seconds maximum
  - Real customer, real location (their business)
  - Hebrew with subtitles
  - Autoplay muted with sound icon visible

- [ ] **PROOF-05**: Real-time proof element
  - Live counter: "לידים שנתפסו השבוע: X"
  - Or: Recent activity feed showing anonymized wins
  - Creates FOMO and social proof simultaneously

- [ ] **PROOF-06**: Metrics that matter to SMB owners
  - NOT vanity metrics (users, downloads)
  - Business metrics: leads captured, reviews generated, calls saved
  - Specific numbers, not percentages without context

- [ ] **PROOF-07**: Case study preview
  - One deep story available
  - Headline visible: "איך [עסק] הכפיל את הלידים שלו ב-3 חודשים"
  - Link to full story (or expand in place)

- [ ] **PROOF-08**: "As seen in" or authority signals
  - If no media coverage yet: industry association logos
  - Or: "מאושר על ידי" certification badges
  - Or: Partner logos (Voicenter, Greeninvoice)

**Certification Gate:** Social proof must achieve 4/4 on D1 (Social Proof) in certification test.

## PHASE C: The Irresistible Offer (Risk Elimination)

> "Make the offer so good that saying no requires effort."

Free trial + Money-back guarantee = COMPLETE risk elimination.
The visitor has NOTHING to lose and EVERYTHING to gain.

### Requirements

- [ ] **OFFER-01**: Free trial messaging prominent
  - "ניסיון חינם" visible on every CTA
  - No credit card required messaging
  - Duration specified (if applicable)

- [ ] **OFFER-02**: Money-back guarantee with name
  - Not just "30-day guarantee"
  - Named guarantee: "ההבטחה של Findo" or "אחריות 100%"
  - Visible near EVERY CTA, not just footer

- [ ] **OFFER-03**: 2-minute setup promise
  - "התקנה ב-2 דקות" badge
  - This addresses time objection directly
  - Must be TRUE and proven in onboarding

- [ ] **OFFER-04**: Zero risk summary block
  - Visual summary of all risk eliminators:
    - ✓ ניסיון חינם
    - ✓ ללא כרטיס אשראי
    - ✓ התקנה ב-2 דקות
    - ✓ החזר כספי מלא

- [ ] **OFFER-05**: Pricing transparency
  - 350 ₪/חודש visible (not hidden)
  - Setup fee explained
  - What's included listed clearly
  - No surprises, no hidden costs

- [ ] **OFFER-06**: ROI calculator
  - "כמה לידים אתה מפסיד?"
  - Input: calls per day, missed call rate
  - Output: potential leads recovered, value in NIS
  - Makes the decision a NO-BRAINER mathematically

- [ ] **OFFER-07**: Comparison with alternatives
  - Cost of doing nothing (lost leads × value)
  - Cost of hiring someone (employee salary)
  - Cost of Findo (350 ₪ = obvious winner)

- [ ] **OFFER-08**: FAQ addressing objections
  - "מה אם זה לא עובד לי?" → Guarantee
  - "כמה זמן לוקח להתקין?" → 2 minutes
  - "האם אני צריך להיות טכני?" → No
  - Each objection answered in 1-2 sentences

**Certification Gate:** E2 (Form Friction) must score full points. D2 (Risk Reversal) must be 4/4.

## PHASE D: The Effortless Action (Zero Friction)

> "Every click should be mindless. Every field costs you conversions."

The action path must be so simple that NOT taking it requires conscious resistance.

### Requirements

- [ ] **ACTION-01**: Primary CTA appears 4-6 times on homepage
  - Above fold
  - After each major section
  - Sticky on mobile (in thumb zone)
  - After testimonials (maximum trust moment)

- [ ] **ACTION-02**: CTA text is value-focused, not action-focused
  - NOT: "הירשם" (Sign up)
  - NOT: "שלח" (Submit)
  - YES: "התחל ניסיון חינם" (Start free trial)
  - YES: "תראה איך זה עובד" (See how it works)

- [ ] **ACTION-03**: What happens next is crystal clear
  - Below CTA: "תוך 2 דקות תהיה מחובר"
  - Or: "3 צעדים פשוטים והעסק שלך עובד בלי שתעשה כלום"
  - Reduce anxiety about the unknown

- [ ] **ACTION-04**: Minimal form fields
  - Free trial: Email + Phone only (2 fields max)
  - Name optional or collected later
  - Each additional field = 5-10% conversion drop

- [ ] **ACTION-05**: Smart form features
  - Israeli phone format auto-detection
  - Email validation inline
  - Hebrew keyboard appropriate
  - Error messages helpful, not accusatory

- [ ] **ACTION-06**: No distractions in conversion flow
  - When CTA is clicked, focus on completion
  - No navigation that leads away
  - No competing actions

- [ ] **ACTION-07**: Mobile CTA optimization
  - Thumb-zone placement (bottom of screen)
  - 48px minimum height
  - Full-width or prominent width
  - Sticky when scrolling

- [ ] **ACTION-08**: Social proof near CTA
  - "הצטרף ל-500+ עסקים" near button
  - Or: Small testimonial snippet
  - Reinforces decision at moment of action

**Certification Gate:** E1 (CTA Analysis) must score 4/4. E2 (Friction Audit) must have 0 friction points.

## PHASE E: The Objection Obliterator (Proactive Handling)

> "Answer the question before they ask it. Silence the doubt before it speaks."

Every common objection must be addressed WITHOUT the visitor needing to ask.

### Requirements

- [ ] **OBJ-01**: "Is this legit?" → Answered by social proof cascade
  - Real names, real faces, real businesses
  - Video testimonials
  - Contact information visible

- [ ] **OBJ-02**: "Can I trust them with my business?" → Answered by transparency
  - Privacy policy easily accessible
  - How data is handled explained
  - WhatsApp integration is THEIR account, we don't access messages

- [ ] **OBJ-03**: "What if it doesn't work for my type of business?" → Answered by variety
  - Testimonials from different industries
  - "Works for" section with business types
  - Or: Quiz to confirm fit

- [ ] **OBJ-04**: "Is it complicated to set up?" → Answered by 2-minute promise
  - Setup time promise repeated
  - Video showing actual setup speed
  - "No technical knowledge required" messaging

- [ ] **OBJ-05**: "What if I need help?" → Answered by support visibility
  - WhatsApp support button (they know WhatsApp)
  - Phone number visible
  - "Real humans, not bots" messaging

- [ ] **OBJ-06**: "Can I cancel if it doesn't work?" → Answered by guarantee
  - "Cancel anytime" messaging
  - Money-back guarantee prominent
  - No lock-in, no penalty

- [ ] **OBJ-07**: "Why should I choose this over others?" → Answered by differentiation
  - "Why Findo" section
  - Key differentiator: "אתה לא עושה כלום - זה עובד לבד"
  - Comparison table if competitors exist

- [ ] **OBJ-08**: "Is it worth the money?" → Answered by ROI calculator
  - Shows value is 10x+ the cost
  - Makes 350 ₪ feel like nothing
  - "Less than one missed lead per month"

**Certification Gate:** FAQ section must address top 5 objections. Each answer must be under 50 words.

Every element on the page must REINFORCE trust, never undermine it.

  - WhatsApp button (Israeli expectation)

  - Physical address or business registration

- [ ] **TRUST-02**: Real team presence
  - "About" or "Who we are" section
  - Real photos, not stock
  - Names and roles
  - Story of why Findo exists

- [ ] **TRUST-03**: Security signals
  - SSL certificate (https)
  - Payment security badges (when relevant)
  - "Your data is safe" messaging

- [ ] **TRUST-04**: Professional presentation
  - No spelling/grammar errors
  - Consistent design language
  - No broken links or images
  - Fast loading

- [ ] **TRUST-05**: Social validation
  - Customer count if significant
  - Reviews/ratings if available
  - Partner logos
  - Media mentions

- [ ] **TRUST-06**: Legal compliance visible
  - Privacy policy link
  - Terms of service link
  - Cookie consent (if required)

- [ ] **TRUST-07**: Guarantee visibility

> "People decide with emotion and justify with logic."

The website must create an EMOTIONAL experience, not just deliver information.

### Requirements

- [ ] **EMOTION-01**: Pain point acknowledgment
  - "You're losing customers right now"
  - "Missed calls = missed money"
  - Make them FEEL the problem

- [ ] **EMOTION-02**: Relief promise
  - "Never worry about missed calls again"
  - "Sleep well knowing every lead is captured"
  - Make them FEEL the solution

- [ ] **EMOTION-03**: Autonomy emphasis
  - "You don't do anything"
  - "It works while you work"
  - "Set it and forget it"
  - Addresses busy SMB owner reality

- [ ] **EMOTION-04**: Success visualization
  - Show the AFTER state
  - Happy business owner
  - Growing business metrics
  - More time with family/life

- [ ] **EMOTION-05**: Micro-interactions that delight
  - Button hover effects that feel good
  - Smooth scroll that feels premium
  - Animations that surprise pleasantly

- [ ] **EMOTION-06**: Copy that speaks to THEM
  - Hebrew that feels natural, not translated
  - Conversational, not corporate
  - "You" focused, not "We" focused

- [ ] **EMOTION-07**: Visual design that conveys premium
  - Clean, modern, professional
  - Colors that feel trustworthy
  - Typography that's readable and pleasant

- [ ] **EMOTION-08**: Achievement moment on signup
  - "You made a great decision"
  - Celebration animation
  - Immediate next step clarity

**Certification Gate:** I1 (Emotional Triggers) must have all 5 emotions present. Copy must pass "Mom Test" for clarity.

## PHASE H: The Live Demo (Show, Don't Tell)

> "A demonstration is worth a thousand words."

Seeing Findo work in real-time eliminates doubt in a way no copy can.

### Requirements

- [ ] **DEMO-01**: Video demo (90 seconds max)
  - Shows complete flow: missed call → WhatsApp → lead captured
  - Real interface, not mockup
  - Hebrew narration with subtitles
  - Professional quality but authentic

- [ ] **DEMO-02**: Interactive demo option
  - Storylane, Navattic, or similar embed
  - Visitor can click through actual interface
  - Guided tour with tooltips
  - Ends with CTA

- [ ] **DEMO-03**: Demo section has clear CTA
  - "Wow" reaction → immediate action path
  - "Start your own trial" button after demo
  - Don't lose the momentum

- [ ] **DEMO-04**: Demo loads fast
  - Lazy load video
  - Thumbnail before play
  - No impact on page LCP

- [ ] **DEMO-05**: Demo works on mobile
  - Video scales properly
  - Interactive demo touch-friendly
  - No horizontal scroll

**Certification Gate:** Demo must be watchable/usable without issues on 3 different devices.

## PHASE I: The Technical Excellence (Performance Foundation)

> "A slow site is a dead site. Users don't wait."

Performance is not a nice-to-have. It directly impacts conversion.

### Requirements

- [ ] **PERF-01**: Lighthouse Performance 95+
  - NOT 90. For 50% conversion, we need 95+
  - Mobile score is primary metric

- [ ] **PERF-02**: LCP under 1.5 seconds
  - NOT 2.5 (Google's "good"). We need excellent.
  - Hero must render fast despite animations

- [ ] **PERF-03**: CLS exactly 0
  - NO layout shift
  - All images have dimensions
  - Fonts don't cause reflow

- [ ] **PERF-04**: INP under 100ms
  - NOT 200 (Google's "good")
  - Interactions must feel instant

- [ ] **PERF-05**: Animation performance
  - 60fps on all animations
  - GPU-accelerated transforms
  - No jank on scroll

- [ ] **PERF-06**: Image optimization
  - WebP/AVIF formats
  - Responsive images (srcset)
  - Lazy loading below fold
  - Blur placeholders

- [ ] **PERF-07**: Font optimization
  - Hebrew font (Heebo) preloaded
  - Font-display: swap
  - Subset if possible

- [ ] **PERF-08**: Code splitting
  - Only load what's needed
  - Dynamic imports for heavy components
  - No unused JavaScript

**Certification Gate:** G1 (Performance) must show green on all Core Web Vitals. PageSpeed Insights 95+.

## PHASE J: The Mobile Mastery (60%+ Traffic)

> "Mobile is not a smaller desktop. It's the primary experience."

Israeli SMB owners live on their phones. Mobile must be PERFECT.

### Requirements

- [ ] **MOBILE-01**: Mobile-first design
  - Designed for mobile, enhanced for desktop
  - NOT desktop scaled down

- [ ] **MOBILE-02**: Touch targets 48px minimum
  - NOT 44px. We need generous targets.
  - Spacing between targets

- [ ] **MOBILE-03**: Thumb-zone CTA
  - Primary action in bottom half of screen
  - Sticky CTA on scroll

- [ ] **MOBILE-04**: Readable without zoom
  - 16px minimum body text
  - Adequate line height (1.5+)
  - Contrast ratio 4.5:1+

- [ ] **MOBILE-05**: Fast on slow connections
  - Under 3 seconds on 3G
  - Graceful degradation of animations

- [ ] **MOBILE-06**: No horizontal scroll
  - Ever. On any page. On any device.

- [ ] **MOBILE-07**: Mobile navigation
  - Hamburger or bottom nav
  - Easy to reach, easy to use

- [ ] **MOBILE-08**: Forms optimized for mobile
  - Appropriate keyboard types
  - Large input fields
  - Minimal scrolling within forms

**Certification Gate:** F1 and F2 must score full points. Real device testing on iPhone + Android required.

## PHASE K: The SEO & Accessibility Foundation

> "Be findable. Be usable by everyone."

### Requirements

- [ ] **SEO-01**: Hebrew meta tags optimized
  - Title: Under 60 characters, keyword + benefit
  - Description: Under 160 characters, compelling

- [ ] **SEO-02**: Structured data
  - Organization schema
  - Product/Service schema
  - FAQ schema
  - Review schema (when reviews exist)

- [ ] **SEO-03**: Technical SEO
  - Sitemap.xml
  - Robots.txt
  - Canonical URLs
  - No duplicate content

- [ ] **SEO-04**: Open Graph & Twitter cards
  - Hebrew image with text
  - Compelling share preview

- [ ] **A11Y-01**: Accessibility basics
  - All images have alt text
  - Semantic HTML (h1, h2, nav, main)
  - Keyboard navigation works
  - Focus indicators visible

- [ ] **A11Y-02**: Screen reader compatible
  - ARIA labels where needed
  - Skip to content link
  - Logical tab order

- [ ] **A11Y-03**: Color contrast
  - 4.5:1 for normal text
  - 3:1 for large text
  - Not relying on color alone

**Certification Gate:** WAVE tool shows 0 errors. SEO audit passes.

## PHASE M: The Certification Gates

> "No page ships without certification. Excellence is not optional."

For 50% conversion, we need EXEMPLARY certification (95+), not just passing (90+).

### Requirements

- [ ] **CERT-01**: Automated certification 95+
  - Run auto-certifier on all pages
  - All 19 automated checks pass

- [ ] **CERT-02**: Manual certification 95+
  - Two independent evaluators
  - All sections score 90%+ of max

- [ ] **CERT-03**: 5-second test perfect
  - 3/3 testers accurate on all 3 questions
  - If any fail, redesign above fold

- [ ] **CERT-04**: User test validation
  - 3 real users complete signup flow
  - Time to completion under 3 minutes
  - No confusion points

- [ ] **CERT-05**: Real device testing
  - iPhone (latest and 2 years old)
  - Android (popular model + budget model)
  - Desktop (Chrome, Safari, Firefox)

- [ ] **CERT-06**: Hebrew native review
  - Native Hebrew speaker reviews all copy
  - Natural tone, not translated
  - No grammatical errors

**Certification Gate:** 95+ overall score before launch. Any lower = iterate until achieved.
