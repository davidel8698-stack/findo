## Panel Container (AutopilotPanels.tsx)

### Desktop Layout (lg+ breakpoint, min-width 1024px)
- Container: `max-w-5xl mx-auto` (~1024px)
- Perspective wrapper: `perspective: 1200px`, `perspectiveOrigin: 50% 40%`
- Inner flex: `transform: rotateX(5deg) rotateY(-3deg)`, `transformStyle: preserve-3d`
- Layout: `display: flex`, `justify-content: center` — RTL direction from html causes first child = rightmost = Reviews
- Panel widths: `flex: 0 0 320px` each
- CRITICAL: No `overflow: hidden`, `filter`, `clip-path`, or `contain: paint` on the perspective wrapper or flex container — these flatten 3D

### Panel Z-Positioning (RTL cascade: front-right to back-left)
- **Reviews (FRONT-RIGHT):** `z-index: 30`, `transform: translateZ(40px)`, shadow: `0 20px 60px rgba(0,0,0,0.3)`
- **Leads (MIDDLE):** `z-index: 20`, `transform: translateZ(0)`, `margin-inline-start: -40px`, `margin-inline-end: -40px`, shadow: `0 25px 70px rgba(0,0,0,0.35)`
- **Activity (BACK-LEFT):** `z-index: 10`, `transform: translateZ(-40px)`, shadow: `0 30px 80px rgba(0,0,0,0.4)`

### Panel Heights (varying for depth perception)
- Reviews (front, largest): natural height, body max-height ~220px overflow hidden
- Leads (middle): body max-height ~200px overflow hidden
- Activity (back, smallest): body max-height ~180px overflow hidden
- The overflow-hidden on body creates the "partially cut off at bottom" effect

### Mobile Layout (below lg breakpoint)
- `lg:hidden space-y-4 px-4`
- All 3 panels full-width, no perspective, no overlap, no Z-transform
- Same content detail as desktop
- Order: Reviews, Leads, Activity (top to bottom)
